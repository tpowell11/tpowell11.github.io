What are rail buttons?

Rail buttons are used to hold model rockets to a launch rail. They are a part of the larger family of components known as launch lugs. This design uses the through-screw style, where a screw passes through the center of the button. In smaller rockets, the screw is just epoxied on the inside of the rocket, rather than using a nut on the inside of the bodytube.

Why Delrin?

Delrin has one of the lowest coefficients of friction against aluminum. It is also readily available through suppliers like McMaster Carr.
